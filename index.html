Temiloluwa (Sweet Valentine❤️❤️❤️)

That smooth taste of a hot coffee—
I can't say I am ignorant of it, or have never tasted it.
Bittersweet, the taste lingers on my tongue.
So sweet I can't get enough of it,
with a little touch of bitter to remind me: we are but human.
​I find you addictive; oh good Lord, what would I do without you?
Bittersweet, keep my emotions at bay.
She's far now; I can't feel her gentle touch now,
yet I can't forget this bittersweet taste.
​I wish we could engage more Love.
I wish we had more time to spend each day.
